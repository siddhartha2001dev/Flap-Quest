# 🎮 Flap Quest

Flap Quest is a fun and engaging browser-based arcade game inspired by the classic Flappy Bird gameplay. Control your character, avoid obstacles, and try to achieve the highest score possible.

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Open the game in your browser.
2. Press **Spacebar** or click to flap.
3. Avoid hitting obstacles.
4. Score points by successfully passing through gaps.
5. Try to beat your highest score.
